We are Team Ascent from Hamburg!
Our team consists of six students from Grootmoor Gymnasium, and together we have one goal: to become World champion. We named our team "Ascent" because the name symbolizes our motivation to rise - the constant drive to develop ourselves, aim higher, and grow beyond our limits together. Our race car is called "Arrow2", reflecting our mindset: precision, speed, and a clear direction - always moving forward. Our logo features two upward-pointing arrowheads that symbolize ascent. At the same time, you can also recognize the "A" from Ascent in the logo. We chose our signature shade of purple, #c53cf5, because it truly sets us apart and embodies the feeling of speed.
