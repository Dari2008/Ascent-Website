Competition

STEM Racing (www.stemracing.com, formerly known as F1 in Schools) is a multi disciplinary international competition in witch students within the age of 11 to 19 design and build a miniature racing car. The car is powered by a CO2 cartridge and has to be build within the rules of the competition. The car is then raced on a 20m long track against other teams. The competition is not only about the car, but also about the presentation of the project. This includes three portfolios, a presentation and a team stand. The competition has been around for many years and is held in many countries around the world. This year it was renamed into STEM Racing.
