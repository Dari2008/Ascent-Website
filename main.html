We are German Vice Champions

STEM Racing is the worlds largest technology competition for students. It focuses on building a miniature Formular 1 car
We are STEM Racing Team Ascent from Hamburg, Germany and are going to the World Finals 2025 in Singapore.

This is our Arrow2, the car that helped us secure the title of the German Vice Champion. It’s the result of constant testing via CAE analysis, wind tunnel testing and real test drives.

We are looking for sponsors for the World Finals in Singapore.

Ascent Rebrand

Ascent goes Magenta! We’ve updated our visual identity. As part of the rebranding, we made minor adjustments to the geometry of our logo to give it a cleaner, more modern look. Alongside this, we’ve changed our main team color from purple to a bold new magenta tone.

These changes are exclusively cosmetic - our passion, values, and goals remain the same.

We are German Vice Champions

On May 17th, we proudly became German Vice Champions and qualified for the Singapore World Finals 2025. The German Championship was an amazing experience. We met lots of kind and inspiring teams, and our car hit a run time of 1.155 seconds. A huge thank you to all our sponsors for making this possible. It was especially great to meet one of them, the Wilhelm Stemmer Stiftung, at the event.

We’re now super excited to continue our journey and head to Singapore with Blue Wolves and Echo Racing. Let’s go Team Germany!

Vorbereitungen für die Deutsche Meisterschaft

After winning the Hamburg Championship, we immediately started preparations for the German Championship the next day. At the kickoff meeting, we established slightly adjusted responsibilities and carried out time and budget planning. Currently, we are actively working on a prototype and sponsor acquisition. We are improving our laser measurement system and making initial decisions regarding our team booth.

We are Hamburger champion!

At the Hamburger championship on the 22th of February 2025 we were able to win the first place and qualify for the German championship. Our car was the fastest of them all with a time of 1.203 seconds. We also won 5 special prices:

  • fastest Car
  • best portfolio enterprise
  • bestes electronic portfolio
  • construction price
  • best presentation
