Sponsoring

To successfully participate in the World Finals, we rely on strong partners and sponsors. The costs for travel, accommodation and, of course, the production of our cars, etc. are significant, and as a student team, we are unable to cover them on our own.

If your company is interested in sponsoring us, please feel free to contact us to discuss the details in person.
